Unit Load Stability

  • Use column stack for strength, interlock for stability - hybrid for heavy.
  • Shrink/stretch wrap at 150-250% prestretch for sustainability.
  • Do not exceed pallet overhang > 25 mm (1") - drops BCT by ~30%.
  • Target pallet height < 180 cm (72 in) for truck/trailer height.
  • Mind lane heat & humidity - de-rate BCT per McKee humidity factor.
