Quick Reference

  • McKee: BCT = 5.874 × ECT × √(h × Z)
  • Dimensional wt divisors: 139 in-lb / 5000 cm-kg
  • Standard atmosphere: 23°C / 50% RH
  • ISO module: 600 × 400 mm
  • GMA pallet: 48 × 40 in

How to use

1. Size your box

Use the McKee BCT calculator with your stack time and RH to predict top-load strength, then find an ECT grade that meets it.

2. Qualify distribution

Pick the correct ISTA procedure (e.g. ISTA 3A for parcel or 6-SIOC for Amazon) and conditioning per ASTM D4332.

3. Design for recycling

Check EU PPWR, APR Design Guide, and state PFAS rules. Favour mono-material films and PCR content.
