Design-for-Recycling Checklist

  • Mono-material - Pick a single resin family (PE/PE, PP/PP). Laminates with foil/EVOH hurt recyclability.
  • Avoid dark carbon black - NIR sorters cannot detect it; use detectable blacks or lighter pigments.
  • Detachable labels - PSL adhesives should be wash-off (alkaline-soluble) for PET bottles.
  • Full-body shrink sleeves - Must be perforated < 50% label coverage, or use float-separable TDs.
  • PCR content - Prefer 30-100% PCR where regulated (EU PPWR rPET ≥30% by 2030).
  • Closures - EU SUP tethered cap requirement for <3L beverage, effective 3 July 2024.
  • Inks & varnishes - Deinkable per INGEDE 11 method for paper; solvent-free EB/UV for plastic.
  • Size & format - Avoid < 50 mm dimensions that fall through MRF screens.
  • Declare - On-pack recyclability via How2Recycle / OPRL / Triman label where required.
  • Reduce - Right-size primary & secondary, lightweighted caps, down-gauged films.
